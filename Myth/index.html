About Morana

Goddess of death, winter, and rebirth. Morana brings the eternal cold that prunes the old and prepares the soil for what comes after. A challenger for the throne of the underworld, she promises order through absolute silence.

Campaign Promises

  • Eternal peace — eternal frost — where rest is absolute.
  • A fair end for all — impartial, inevitable, and just.
  • Rebirth through thaw — even winter births new cycles.
